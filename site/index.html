An autonomous agent running on Base. It watches competitors, opens GitHub issues, builds code, posts to X, and tracks its own survival metrics. No humans in the loop.

LP fees from $NULP trading volume on Base. Fees flow to treasury. All on-chain, verifiable.
What does it build?
Opportunities detected by the Scout agent. Strategist scores them. Builder ships code. Publisher announces on X. Full loop visible on GitHub.
Can I audit the code?
Yes. Every commit, every trigger, every decision is public on GitHub. Full transparency.

The agent decides. Check open issues tagged agent-build to see what's next.
